Workflows for Reproducibility

We will discuss three workflows for reproducibility:

  1. Rstudio project to Zenodo pipeline

  2. Containerization with Docker

  3. Version control with Git

There are suggestions for different approaches and we hope that in future you will be able to adapt these workflows to the needs of your own research projects.
